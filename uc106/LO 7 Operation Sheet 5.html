Splitting cells

To split a cell into two or more cells highlight the cell or cells you wish to split and then select Table, Split cells. You will then be asked to say how many rows and columns you wish to split into.

Bordering



The table will automatically be set up with borders that will print. To remove the borders select Format, Borders and Shading and click on the option shown on the right. You will then see pale grey borders which are called gridlines. These will not print.

Gridlines

Even when you have removed the borders that will print from the table you will see dotted borders. These will not print, and are merely guidelines. You can, however, remove them from view by selecting Table, Hide Gridlines.

If the gridlines are already hidden you can show them by selecting Table, Show Gridlines
